Questions

Before you ask

Which phones does the X6 work with?

Magnetic wireless charging works out of the box with iPhone 12 and newer. Any phone with Qi wireless charging can use it with a magnetic ring or case, and every phone can charge over the Type-C port at up to 20W.

Can I take it on a plane?

Yes. Both capacities are well under the 100 Wh airline limit and the X6 is UN38.3 certified for battery transport. Keep it in your carry-on, as airlines require for all power banks.

Can I use my phone while it charges?

Yes — the X6 sits flush against the back of your phone, so you can text, scroll and shoot photos while it tops you up. The aluminum body also spreads heat, so it stays comfortable to hold.

How do I recharge the X6 itself?

Through the same Type-C port, with bidirectional 20W PD — the 5000 mAh model refills in about an hour and a half. The LED display shows the charge level while it fills.

What's the warranty and return policy?

Every X6 ships with an 18-month warranty. If it isn't for you, return it within 30 days in its original condition for a full refund.
